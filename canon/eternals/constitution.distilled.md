# ETERNALS Constitution — distilled

Source (public Notion):
- https://neweden1.notion.site/The-Constitution-of-the-ETERNALS-Society-57e054182b1144609a49336da3c27fcc

## Preamble (intent)
A happy and free society; happiness/development/needs of every citizen; commitment to defend the principles and values in this constitution.

## Article 1 — Basic principles (operational)
- Contractual jurisdiction: rights/obligations via voluntary agreements.
- Self‑ownership.
- Non‑Aggression Principle (NAP) + peaceful conflict resolution; self-defense only.
- Consensus decision-making (no coercion; align maps; sustain joint action).
- Equality/justice; equal rights/opportunities.
- Public ownership of resources (knowledge/tech/material goods) for common wellbeing.
- Guaranteed access to info/education/healthcare/comfortable living (food/clothing/housing).
- Freedom of development/self-realization; respect unique interests/abilities.
- Freedom of thought/expression/consciousness.
- Maximum transparency/openess: use all available information to improve quality of life.
- Sexual freedom/diversity; open relationships by mutual consent.
- Children are artificially created and raised in specialized social structures.
- Ecological sustainability; rejects use of animals.

## Article 2 — Development priorities
Biotech (anti-aging), neurotech (choose states/cognition), cybernetics (bodies/mind upload), AI support system, eco-tech, VR spaces, artificial creation of new citizens; priorities via public consensus/direct vote.

## Article 3 — Governance
Councils formed by voluntary participation + confirmed competencies; decisions by consensus; no personal privileges.

## Article 4 — Foreign relations
Self-determination of other societies; non-interference/non-aggression; friendly relations; free trade/knowledge exchange; comply with treaties.
